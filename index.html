01 — Brand Overview

Who We Are

EchoLift is a platform built around community, health, and the act of giving. Our visual identity should always feel warm, trustworthy, and energetic.

🎯

Brand Personality

Warm, energetic, trustworthy, and human. We're serious about impact but always approachable in how we communicate and design.

✨

Visual Promise

Every design we put out should feel intentional, joyful, and reflect the warmth of human connection — never cold or corporate.

02 — Logo

Logo Usage

✓ Do

  • Use the logo on approved backgrounds only
  • Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "E"
  • Use the full lockup (icon + wordmark) where possible
  • Scale the logo proportionally
  • Use icon-only version for small sizes (app icons, favicons)

✗ Don't

  • Stretch, distort, or rotate the logo
  • Change the logo colors to unapproved colors
  • Add drop shadows or effects to the logo
  • Place logo on busy or clashing backgrounds
  • Retype the wordmark in a different font

05 — Imagery

Photography & Illustration

Our visuals tell human stories. We blend real photography with clean illustrations — always warm, always authentic, always people-first.

Real People, Real Moments

Use authentic African faces in real contexts — healthcare workers, families, communities. Avoid overly staged stock photos.

Brand-Colored Overlays

Apply coral overlays or primary-colored backgrounds to unify photography with brand identity, especially for campaign posts.

Bold & Conceptual

For campaign launches, bold conceptual imagery (like our "master" character) with strong graphic compositions creates impact.

Flat Illustrations

Use brand-colored flat illustrations for health campaigns, celebrations, and awareness posts. Keep style consistent.

Supplementary Accents OPTION

Leaf, plant, and nature motifs (as seen in World Health Day design) can supplement health-focused content for added warmth.

ECHOLIFT_APP

Branded Templates

All images posted on social must include the EchoLift logo (top-left or top-center) and the @ECHOLIFT_APP handle in the footer.

📸 Photo Selection Criteria

  • Warm, natural lighting — avoid cold or harsh studio flash looks
  • Authentic expressions — genuine smiles and candid moments
  • Diverse African representation across age groups and regions
  • Clear focal subject — avoid cluttered or distracting backgrounds
  • High resolution (minimum 1080×1080px for social)
  • Avoid watermarked or heavily filtered stock imagery

🚫 What to Avoid

  • Images that portray poverty or suffering without dignity
  • Non-African faces as primary subjects (unless campaign-specific)
  • Heavily desaturated or black-and-white photos (unless intentional)
  • Overcrowded designs with too many images at once
  • Pixelated or low-resolution images
  • Generic stock photo "handshake" corporate imagery
